About Miraculum Films

Miraculum Films is a visionary media production company dedicated to creating captivating content across multiple platforms. We specialize in bringing stories to life through web series, podcasts, short films, feature films, and commercials.

Our team of skilled professionals combines artistic vision with technical expertise to deliver exceptional content that resonates with audiences. From concept development to final delivery, we handle every aspect of production with meticulous attention to detail.

Whether you need a compelling commercial to showcase your brand, a feature film to tell your story, or anything in between, Miraculum Films has the experience, talent, and resources to make it happen.

THE LAST WAVE

A haunting journey through grief and vengeance, The Last Wave follows mute cook Todi and paralyzed Victor. Secrets emerge, revealing twisted pasts and devastating truths. As love and betrayal collide, the story ends with a powerful, unforgettable twist.

SHORT FILMS

LUNCH BREAK

During his chaotic lunch in the park, office worker Graham encounters Larry, an unpredictable stranger. Mishaps unfold—crossword errors, coffee spills, stolen briefcases. Amid frustration, laughter and connection emerge as Graham shares his sandwich, discovering unexpected friendship through disorder.

SHORT FILMS

LETTER ACROSS THE CHANNEL

Three journalists from the UK, Germany, and Nigeria debate how to cover the Ukraine wars ripple effects—whether to spotlight individual struggles or reveal global economic ties—while navigating national bias, journalistic duty, and the search of understanding.

SHORT FILMS

CLIFF WALK

After suffering constant bullying, Jason confronted his stalker with only a pen. Instead of retaliating, he showed restraint and let him go—demonstrating strength, control, and a refusal to become what once hurt him. A moment of true transformation.

SHORT FILMS

THE GAP

In the unrest of the 1900s, a factory explosion leaves Jacob blamed for sabotage. Betrayed and pursued, his fate entangles with Mia and Sarah. Time-traveler Mathew returns home, uncovering a haunting link between their tragedy and his present.

Meet Our Team

Our talented professionals combine creativity, technical expertise, and passion to deliver exceptional media productions that exceed expectations.

Snehal Runwal

Director, Writer, Producer

Snehal M Runwal is a versatile filmmaker, serving as a director, writer, and producer with six years of experience in media, spanning filmmaking, journalism, and business. Based at The Miraculum Films, Snehal’s approach to storytelling is driven by a passion for exploring the human mind, crafting immersive narratives that blend suspense, emotion, and visual artistry to provoke introspection and captivate audiences. Their educational background includes a Master of Arts in Direction, Film and Television from Bournemouth University, a Master of Arts in Mass Communication from Sikkim Manipal University, and an MBA from ICFAI University, alongside specialized courses in screenwriting and filmmaking. Snehal’s portfolio showcases diverse projects from 2023-2025, including web series like The Perfect Immigrants and The Great NRI Adventures, which explore immigrant experiences in the UK with humor and depth. Short films such as The Last Wave delve into themes of love, loss, and revenge, while Lunch Break offers comedic storytelling. Commercials for local Bournemouth businesses like Hotel Celebrity and Vaigai Supermarket highlight their commercial versatility. Snehal’s work reflects a commitment to thought-provoking, emotionally resonant storytelling across genres, blending cultural insights with cinematic innovation.

Regina Finch

As the Managing Director of Miraculum Films, Regina excels in film production, leveraging her expertise in producing, line producing, project management, and creative vision.
